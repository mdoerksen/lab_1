Overwatch 2 Character Overview: Ashe

Introduction

Ashe is a bold and ambitious gunslinger in Overwatch 2, known for her leadership of the Deadlock Gang. Armed with her lever-action rifle, the Viper, and supported by her loyal Omnic companion, B.O.B., Ashe excels at mid-range combat. She has a sharp mind for strategy and a rebellious streak, making her a formidable foe on the battlefield.
